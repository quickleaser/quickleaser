Description:

Ludwig's Castle is the final level in the Twin Bridges area of Super Mario World. It can be accessed by completing Cookie Mountain or Butter Bridge 2. By completing this level, the player gains access to Forest of Illusion 1. It consists of a maze-like narrow corridor, a large room with a descending spiked ceiling, and a tall chamber containing many fences with Climbing Koopas, and a boss fight with Ludwig von Koopa at the very top of the castle.

After Mario reaches Ludwig's room, he must avoid the four fireballs that Ludwig will spit out. Jumping on him will make him spin across the floor in his shell. After he stops, Ludwig will leap out to the opposite side. Jumping on him 2 more times will send him flying into the air where he explodes.
